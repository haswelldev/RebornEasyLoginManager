Everything you need to juggle your accounts

Designed for players who run many characters — mains, buffers, crafters and alts.

🗂️

Unlimited alts

Add, edit, duplicate and delete up to 199 accounts per file, each with its own description.

✋

Drag to reorder

Grab any row and drag it — a floating preview follows your cursor and an insertion line shows exactly where it lands, with edge auto-scroll for long lists.

🔎

Instant search

Filter accounts by login or description as you type. Find the right alt in a second.

📋

Copy password safely

Right-click any account to copy its password to the clipboard — without ever revealing it on screen.

⌨️

Keyboard-first

A full menu bar with shortcuts: open, save, add, rename, delete and reorder without touching the mouse.

💾

Never lose changes

Unsaved edits are tracked with a title-bar marker and you're always prompted before closing or switching files.

🌍

Speaks your language

Fully translated into English, French, Greek, Portuguese, Polish, Ossetian and Chinese.

🧩

Truly portable

On Windows it's a single .exe with no installer and no dependencies. On macOS, a clean native app bundle.

Privacy by design

Your passwords stay yours.

Easy Login Manager works 100% offline. It only reads and writes the local .ini file that the L2 Reborn client already uses. There is no server, no account, no telemetry and no network connection of any kind — your credentials are never uploaded, synced or shared with anyone.

And because the entire app is open source, you don't have to take our word for it. Anyone can read exactly what it does.

Made for L2 Reborn

Easy Login Manager understands the [L2REBORN_EASYLOGIN] account file used by the L2 Reborn client, so your logins are ready the moment you launch the game. New to the server? Head over to l2reborn.org to get started.
